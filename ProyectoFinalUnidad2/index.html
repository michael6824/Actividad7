What is a Pet?

A pet, or companion animal, is an animal kept primarily for a person's company or entertainment rather than as a working animal, livestock or a laboratory animal.

All the animals are Pets?

NO!, while many people have kept many different species of animals in captivity over the course of human history, only a relative few have been kept long enough to be considered domesticated.
The wild animals are recognized as not suitable to keep as pets, and this practice is completely banned in many places.

Health benefits

It is widely believed among the public, and among many scientists, that pets probably bring mental and physical health benefits to their owners.
